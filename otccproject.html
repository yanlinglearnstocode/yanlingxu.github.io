Olive Tree Campus Church Website - Community Love

Olive Tree Campus Church is a student church based in Ann Arbor, Michigan aiming to serve students and scholars at the University of Michigan and the local community. Established in 2012, Olive Tree Campus Church is very young and unique, trying to help more students better understand belief. As a member of the church, I volunteered to be the designer and developer for the website of the church and successfully launched the website in December, 2016.

About the Website

Initially all the activities were announced within our social network accounts. However, as the church community grows larger, there is an increasing need in broadening our influence in the community and providing better access for people who want to visit our church. I made the aaotcc.org website as a central hub for our regular services and events, stories & photos of past events, and a contact form for general questions or inquiries.

Highlights of the Project

When redesigning the website, I reorganized the content, simplified the structure, and highlighted the key features.
