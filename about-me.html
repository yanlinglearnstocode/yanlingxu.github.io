Experienced Data Cruncher, Critical Thinker, New Designer

Hello, I am Yanling Xu.

Currently I do survey sample management, database construction and statistical analysis at Health and Retirement Study in Institute for Social Research - the largest social science institute in the US.
My aspiration for UX design career began as a researcher, where I maintained sample database of more than 10000 respondents, designed crosswalk data tables of 2 age cohorts, and generated individual retirement wealth data for public release. Eventually, I realized that I wanted to be designing solutions to problems rather than analyzing them alone. Otherwise, I consider myself a self-taught designer.

I am seeking opportunities to return to school and untertake graduate studies in Human Centered Design and Engineering field.
